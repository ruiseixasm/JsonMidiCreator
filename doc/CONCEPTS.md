# Writing
## Literal
* Linear with direct interpretation;
* Rigid, difficult to manipulate.
## Mathematical
* Irregular with no direct interpretation;
* Flexible, highly manipulatable.
# Duty Level and Precedence
The higher the duty level the higher the precedence, thus, `*` shall represent more actions than the `+`, as `<<` should represent less actions than `+`.
Heavy-duty operations are extensive and repetitive, coarse, low-duty operations are specific and sporadic, fine. Macro vs Micro operations.
